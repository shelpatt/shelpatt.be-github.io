THE FOUNDERS

JULIA & FLORENT

The company was founded in 2013 by Julia and Florent, the couple who has always been passionate about desserts. It all started when they moved in together and Julia was always spoiling her husband with desserts. They both loved this so much that after a year, they decided to make a business out of their hobby.
